 Disk

     A magnetic storage medium designed for long-term storage.  Disks come in
     two varieties: hard disks (fast but fixed) or floppy disks (slow but
     removable).  A disk can be partitioned into multiple volumes, each
     containing a tree-structured directory system that holds files
     accessible by programs.
