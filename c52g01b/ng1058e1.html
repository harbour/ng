 Drive

     (file) A disk drive or a letter (normally followed by a colon) that
     designates a disk drive.  On most computers, the letters A and B refer
     to floppy disk drives; other letters refer to fixed disk drives or
     logical drives (e.g., fixed disk partitions or network drives).
