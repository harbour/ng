 Identifier

     (preprocessor, procedure and function, variable) A name that identifies
     a function, procedure, variable, constant or other named entity in a
     source program.  In CA-Clipper, identifiers must begin with an
     alphabetic character and may contain alphabetic characters, numeric
     characters, and the underscore character.
