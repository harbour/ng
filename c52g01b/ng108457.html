 Hexadecimal

     (general) A representation of a value in base 16 rather than decimal
     which is base 10.  Hexadecimal values are easily converted to and from
     binary (base 2) which is the form of data the computer actually uses.
     Hexadecimal values are represented by digits zero through nine and A
     through F for values between 10 and 15.
