 Retry

     (network) Upon failing to lock a record in a database that is opened in
     shared mode, retry refers to attempting the write again based upon
     certain programmatic parameters.

     (error handling) After an exception has been raised and the conditions
     of a failure corrected, an attempt is made to reexecute the failed
     operation.
