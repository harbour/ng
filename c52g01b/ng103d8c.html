 Control Structure

     (language) Any program structure that alters the flow of program
     control.  In CA-Clipper, these include:

     .  BEGIN SEQUENCE...END

     .  DO WHILE...ENDDO

     .  DO CASE...ENDCASE

     .  FOR...NEXT

     .  IF...ENDIF
