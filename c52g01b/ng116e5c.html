 Word

     (preprocessor) A series of characters in a match pattern or result
     pattern.  Source text matches a word in a match pattern if the text is
     identical to the word or is an acceptable abbreviation of it.  A word
     that appears in a result pattern is copied unmodified into the result
     text.
