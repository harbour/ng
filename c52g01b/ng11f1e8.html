 Relational Operators
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 Operator      | Description
               |
 <             | Less than
 >             | Greater than
 =             | Equal
 ==            | Exactly equal for character; equal for other data types
 <>, #, or !=  | Not equal
 <=            | Less than or equal
 >=            | Greater than or equal
 $             | Is contained in the set or is a subset of
