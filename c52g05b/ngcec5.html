 140 NEW: Directives
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     The Clipper 5.0 compiler has a new pre-processor feature that
     supports the following directives:

     #command | #translate
     #define
     #ifdef...#else...#endif
     #ifndef...#else...#endif
     #include
     #undef
