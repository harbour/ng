 76  CHANGED: SETCANCEL()
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     In Clipper 5.0, Ctrl-Break can be used to terminate a program in
     the same manner as Alt-C.  SETCANCEL() controls the ability of
     Ctrl-Break to terminate a program.
