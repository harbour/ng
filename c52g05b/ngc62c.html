 135 CHANGED: PARAMETERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     In Clipper 5.0, the PARAMETERS statement assigns a NIL value for
     missing command-line parameters.  In Summer '87, missing PARAMETERS
     retain their previous value.
