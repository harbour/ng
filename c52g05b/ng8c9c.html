 92  CHANGED: @...PROMPT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     The maximum number of prompts that you can define with @...PROMPT
     has been increased from 32 to 4096.  The new limitation is due to
     the fact that 4096 is the maximum number of elements in an array
     which is how the prompts are stored.
