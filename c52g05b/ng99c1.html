 105 CHANGED: NOTE*
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     The NOTE* command is now obsolete with the addition of C-style
     comments to the Clipper language.  For multi-line comments,
     slash-asterisk (/*) marks the beginning of the comment block and
     asterisk-slash (*/) marks the end.  The double-slash (//) can be
     used for single-line and in-line comments.
