 75  NEW: TBrowse:colWidth()
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     TBrowse:colWidth() is a new method that returns the display width
     of a specified column contained in the TBrowse object.  If the
     specified column is out of bounds or not supplied or not a number,
     the method returns zero.
