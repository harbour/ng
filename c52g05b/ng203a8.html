 135 CHANGED:  Monitor Menu
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     The Monitor menu opens a separate window for monitored variables
     rather than displaying them in the Watch Window.  The new window is
     called the Monitor Window and it remains open on the main Debugger
     screen as long as variables are being monitored.  The Monitor
     Window may be selected in order to inspect monitored variables.
