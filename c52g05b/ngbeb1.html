 131 NEW: FIELD
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     FIELD is a new statement that declares database field names to the
     compiler and optionally supplies an implicit alias for the fields.
     If an alias is not specified in the FIELD statement, unaliased
     references to FIELD variables are implicitly preceded by the field
     alias (FIELD->).
