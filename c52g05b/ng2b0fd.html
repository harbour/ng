 CHANGED:  BP command
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     An additional parameter, <nLineNum> has been added to the BP command.

     BP [<nLineNum> [<cFileName>]]|[<cFuncName>]

     The breakpoint is toggled at <nLineNum> in the current file.
