 69  CHANGED: READKEY()*
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     The READKEY() function, originally provided as a sample program, is
     now part of EXTEND.LIB.  READKEY(), however, is now classified as a
     compatibility function, and it is recommended that you use
     LASTKEY() instead.
