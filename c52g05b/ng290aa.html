 NEW:      TBrowse:forceStable()
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     The new method forceStable() performs a full stabilization of the
     TBrowse.  It is analogous to the following code, only slightly faster:

     DO WHILE .NOT. oBrowse:stabilize()
     ENDDO
