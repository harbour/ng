 136 REMOVED: Callstack Menu
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     The Callstack menu has been removed.  Callstack:Open and
     Callstack:Close were replaced by the single View:Callstack menu
     command.  Callstack:Inspect is accomplished by selecting the
     Callstack Window when it is open on the main Debugger screen.
