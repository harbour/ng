 68  NEW: Get:colorDisp()
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     Get:colorDisp() is a new method that changes the colors of a Get
     object and then redisplays it.  The method is exactly equivalent to
     assigning Get:colorSpec and issuing Get:display().
