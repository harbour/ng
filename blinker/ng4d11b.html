 1179: too many entries in packed symbol table
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 Blinker has reached an internal size limit while compressing the symbol
 table of a CA-Clipper application. This is extremely unlikely to occur in
 normal operation.

 Please contact Blinker technical support with your serial number and exact
 details of the circumstances causing this error.
