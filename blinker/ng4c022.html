 1169: DPMI memory allocation error
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 This message indicates that there was an error allocating memory from the
 DPMI host, and should not happen in normal circumstances. Please contact
 Blink, Inc technical support with your Blinker serial number and exact
 details of the circumstances causing the error.
