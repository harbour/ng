 1213: attempt to call DEFINED routine
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 The link script command has been used to 'stub out' a particular routine,
 but a call to the routine has been made during execution of the program.
 Either remove all calls to the defined routine from the program, or remove
 the DEFINE command from your link script.
