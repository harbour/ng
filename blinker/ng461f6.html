 1114: <file(module)> : '<symbol>' duplicated in <file(module)>
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 The same information applies as for warning error message number 1004, but
 because of the nature of the duplication, linking cannot continue. To
 resolve this error, give one of the symbols a different name and recompile
 the appropriate .OBJ file.
