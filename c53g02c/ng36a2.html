 C2004   Illegal character

     Explanation:  You specified an illegal character within a program
     statement and not within a literal string.  Legal characters for
     identifiers and keywords include (_, a-z, A-Z, ., ., ., .,
     ., ., .) for the first character followed by (_, a-z, A-Z, 0-9,
     ., ., ., ., ., ., .).

     Action:  Remove the offending character from the program statement
     and recompile.
