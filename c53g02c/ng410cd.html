 9006    DBFNTX RDD index buffer access error

     Explanation:  The runtime system has failed to detect an RDD loaded
     in an application that requires one.

     Action:  Check third party .LIBraries for compatibility and version.
     Check link script and order.  Look for missing RDD .LIBrary or .OBJ
     files.  The .EXE may be invalid.

     See Also:  Errors 999 and 1011 in this chapter, "Linking" and
     "Debugger" chapters of the Programming and Utilities Guide.
