 C2066   Unclosed optional clause in #translate/#command

     Explanation:  You specified a #translate/#xtranslate or
     #command/#xcommand directive containing an optional clause with a
     missing right square bracket.  All optional clauses must be enclosed in
     square brackets.

     Action:  Add the missing square bracket and recompile.
