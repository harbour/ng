 DBCMD/1001   Argument error: SEEK or DBSEEK

     Explanation:  The argument to the SEEK or DBSEEK() was non-numeric,
     logical, character, or date.

     Action:  Correct the program.

     See Also:  DBSEEK() function, SEEK command
