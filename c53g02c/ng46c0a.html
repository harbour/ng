 DBG0101 Unable to disconnect from Windows/DOS communications Pipe.

     Explanation: CA-Clipper Workbench could not close the CA-Clipper
     application DOS window.

     Action:  The DOS window must be closed by using the window's control
     box and selecting the CLOSE menu item.
