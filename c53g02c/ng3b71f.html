 704     Screen display driver I/O error upon startup

     Explanation:  The CRT detected was not compatible for I/O access.

     Action:  Make sure the latest CA-Clipper libraries are used, and
     that no third-party libraries are linked.  Determine that the .EXE is
     not corrupted.  Determine the screen may be accessed from the DOS
     prompt.
