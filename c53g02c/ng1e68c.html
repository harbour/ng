 1127:   near communals segment exceeds 64kb

     Explanation:  The total size of the NEAR communal variables segment
     exceeds the maximum permissible size (64Kb).

     Action:  To reduce the size of the segment change some NEAR
     uninitialized data declarations to FAR in the program source.
