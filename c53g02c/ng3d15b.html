 1201    No master index in use

     Explanation:  An operation, such as a SEEK, was performed in the
     work area but there was no master or controlling index order.

     Action:  Make sure that the index ORDER is not ZERO, or nullified,
     or that the TAG that had been SET ORDER TO was not deleted within a
     multi-tag index.
