 C2040   Invalid unary inline operator

     Explanation:  The expression in question is not specified correctly.

     Action:  Examine the expression carefully and correct any obvious
     mistakes.  Also, use parentheses to clarify the expression.  This
     increases readability and makes problems more evident.
