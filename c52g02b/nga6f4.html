 C2070   #else does not match #ifdef

     Explanation:  You specified an #else directive that does not fall
     within a #ifdef or #ifndef control structure.

     Action:  Check to see that each #else directive you specified is
     nested between an #ifdef or #ifndef and an #endif directive, correct and
     recompile.
