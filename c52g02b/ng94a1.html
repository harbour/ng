 C2058   Comma or right parenthesis missing in #define

     Explanation:  You specified a pseudofunction definition with a
     missing comma or right parenthesis.  When you define a pseudofunction,
     you must specify matching left and right parentheses as well as separate
     all function arguments with commas.

     Action:  Check the definition closely, supply the missing character,
     and recompile.
