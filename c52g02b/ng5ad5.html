 C2020   Mistreatment of CALLed symbol

     Explanation:  A previously CALLed procedure has been invoked with
     either a DO statement or a function call.

     Action:  Make sure that routines you intend to CALL do not have the
     same names as CA-Clipper routines defined in your program.
