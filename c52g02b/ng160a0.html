 ert0086 Cannot read RTLINK.DAT data (freeformat parser data)

     Explanation:  .RTLink was able to locate RTLINK.DAT, but an error
     occurred while reading the file data.

     Action:  RTLINK.DAT is probably corrupted, and you will need to
     reinstall it from your distribution disks.
