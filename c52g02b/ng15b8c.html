 ert0078 Error reading RTLINKST.COM

     Explanation:  .RTLink was able to locate RTLINKST.COM, but an error
     occurred while reading the file.

     Action:  RTLINKST.COM is probably corrupted, and you will need to
     reinstall it from your distribution disks.
