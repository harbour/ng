  Is the SIx Driver protected mode compatible?:

  Yes!  The SIx Driver can be linked with protected mode linkers such as
  Blinker 3.x, ExoSpace, and CauseWay with no special configuration
  requirements or extra files required (other than those required by the
  linker itself, if any).
