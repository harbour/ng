  When should I use optimization?:

  Absolutely everywhere!  Mach SIx will optimize anywhere possible.  If the
  query is not-optimizable, Mach SIx will simply turn control over to
  Clipper for processing.  But when it is, you'll be amazed at the speed
  differences.
