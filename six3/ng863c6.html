  Can I optimize queries using DBFNTX or SIXNTX?:

  No.  The DBFNTX and SIXNTX data drivers are not supported in this version
  of Mach SIx.  You must be in a SIXCDX or SIXNSX workarea to optimize
  queries with Mach SIx.
