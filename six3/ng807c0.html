  Can I use the same files simultaneously with FoxPro on a network?:

  Yes!  The SIXCDX (FoxPro-compatible) flavor of the SIx Driver automatically
  supports FoxPro's record-locking scheme, allowing your SIXCDX application
  to access data and indexes concurrently with a FoxPro application.
