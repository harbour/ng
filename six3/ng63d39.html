  SIx Driver Features:

  This section outlines the SIx Driver's major features.  The following is
  a quick rundown of what the SIx Driver has to offer:

    .  Four (4) RDD's in ONE!  (SIXCDX, SIXNSX, SIXMDX, and SIXNTX)

    .  Built-in Mach SIx Query Optimizer

    .  HiPer-SEEK (Integrated Fast Text Search Technology)

    .  VariFields (tm) (New "V" field types compact your data!)

    .  100% FoxPro File-Format Compatibility (SIXCDX)

    .  FoxPro record-locking support (SIXCDX)

    .  Compact Indexes (SIXCDX and SIXNSX)

    .  Compound Indexes (SIXCDX, SIXNSX, and SIXMDX)

    .  Roll-Your-Own Indexes (SIXCDX and SIXNSX)

    .  Multi-Key, Free-Format Indexes (SIXCDX and SIXNSX)

    .  Conditional and Scoped Indexes

    .  Sub-Indexes

    .  Ordinal Index Position Functions (SIXCDX and SIXNSX)

    .  Definable Memo Block Sizes (SIXCDX and SIXNSX)

    .  Multiple-Record Locking

    .  Record Edit Rollbacks  (No more writing scatter / gather routines!)

    .  Maximum .FPT/.SMT memo file size of 4Gb

    .  Array storage in FPT/SMT memo fields

    .  FPT/SMT memo files reuse discarded blocks (Reduces memo bloat!)

    .  MEMOPACK command/function for FPT/SMT/DBT memo files

    .  Data Encryption

    .  Data Compression

    .  Data Dictionary (.INI) Support

    .  Database Event Triggers

    .  100% Compatible with protected mode linkers (Blinker 3.x, ExoSpace,
       and CauseWay)

    .  100% Compatible with R&R Report Writer

    .  RDD Utilities
