  SIX???/642  Key Not In Index

  Problem:   The key that is being removed (using Sx_KeyDrop()) was not
             found in the index.

  Solution:  This is an informational warning.  If your program depends
             on the key actually being removed, make sure the key exists in
             the index before trying to remove it.
