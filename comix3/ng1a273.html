I'm moving from ClipMore/NTX to /CMX & have Unresolved Externals!  Why?
    
The commands and functions in ClipMore/CMX and ClipMore/NTX are different.
Some of the commands and functions implemented in ClipMore/NTX are no longer
necessary in ClipMore/CMX.  ClipMore/CMX uses a RecordList instead of a
temporary index to isolate records meeting the filter condition.

Here are the missing functions with their equivalents:

    Old Function        New Replacement
    ------------        ---------------
    cmClose()           USE
    cmCloseDB()         CLOSE DATABASES
    cmCloseAll()        CLOSE ALL
    cmGetIndCnt()       cmxIndexCnt()
    cmIndex()           INDEX ON .. FOR
    cmIndexOrd()        indexOrd()
    cmAddIndex()        dbSetIndex()
    cmSetOrder()        dbSetOrder() or ordSetFocus()
