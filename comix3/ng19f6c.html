What are LoadStone's support and update policies?

BBS access and support is provided for a period of 12 months from the date
of purchase of the software at no charge.

Voice support is provided at no charge for 30 days from the _first_ call to
Technical Support.

Additional voice support beyond this period is available at $75/hour
billable in 1 minute increments.

Patches for minor upgrades are available on the BBS at no charge.

See Appendix C: Technical Support and Appendix D: Using the BBS & Obtaining
Updates for more information.
